Wellness Monitor

Wellness is a smart module designed to monitor a patient's overall health status. It evaluates key factors like sleep quality, stress level, heart rate, and daily calorie intake. Our AI model analyzes these inputs to determine if the person is well-rested and healthy. This feature empowers Thalassemia patients to track lifestyle habits that impact recovery. It promotes preventive care and supports personalized health recommendations.
